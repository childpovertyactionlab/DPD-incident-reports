1 City of Dallas Overview

Main Takeaways

The violent crime rate for November 2021 was 1.165 per 10,000 residents.

In the month of November 2021 there was a total of 152 violent gun crimes across the city.

This is a decrease of -16.5% from October 2021, and a decrease of -18.3% from November 2020.

There were 11 incidents with 4 or more victims across the city.

20.4% of all violent gun crimes in October occurred in South Central Division

28.9% (44) of Violent Gun Crimes occurred within a cell identified as “High Risk” by our most recent Risk Terrain Modeling Analysis.
