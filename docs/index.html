Main Takeaways

The violent crime rate for January 2022 was 1.388 per 10,000 residents.

In the month of January 2022 there was a total of 181 violent gun crimes across the city.

This is an increase of 11% from December 2022, and a decrease of -6.2% from January 2021.

There were 5 incidents with 4 or more victims across the city.

19.3% of all violent gun crimes in October occurred in Northeast Division

33.7% (61) of Violent Gun Crimes occurred within a cell identified as “High Risk” by our most recent Risk Terrain Modeling Analysis.
